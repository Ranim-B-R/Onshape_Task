# Onshape_Task
A 3D-printable Pikachu-head keychain, designed from scratch in OnShape.

## How it was made
1. a profile was sketched of one side of Pikachu's face. after that the picture was mirrored and defined to complete the sketch

![OnShape sketch of the Pikachu head and ear profile, showing dimensions such as a 28mm width, 50mm height, and a 30mm diameter circle for the head, with Rho 0.5 fillets defining the curves](pikachu-sketch-profile.png)

![OnShape sketch with full dimension callouts for the ears, eyes, cheeks, and mouth details of the Pikachu head](pikachu-sketch-dimensions.png)

2. The finished 3D model, complete with color-separated features and a keyring hole.
 
![Finished 3D render of the Pikachu keychain in OnShape, showing the yellow head, black-tipped ears, red cheeks, black eyes, and pink mouth, with a small hole for attaching a keyring](pikachu-keychain-final.png)
